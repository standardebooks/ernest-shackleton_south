Shackleton uses an unusual way to include quotes from his logs and his men’s diaries, often starting such a quote part way through a paragraph, then followed by several more paragraphs of the remainder of the quote. In order to semantically tag such quotes, I have therefore taken the liberty of inserting a paragraph break before such quotes and blockquoting the entire quote with appropriate semantic tags. This was done as a separate [Editorial] commit so it is reversable if necessary.
